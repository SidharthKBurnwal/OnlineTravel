About Us

Our History

Started in 2010, Tourister quickly established itself as a travelling icon par excellence in India. With its unique brand of world fusion Tour management sense that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in India. Featuring the best experiences you can get in a tour, you never know what will arrive on your plate the next time you visit us.

The company traces its humble beginnings to The Indian Tourist, a successful chain started by our CEO, Mr. Sidharth Kumar Burnwal, that featured for the first time the world's best Tourist places.
